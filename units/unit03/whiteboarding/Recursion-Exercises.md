## Recursion Exercises

<pre> 
1. Find the factorial of a given number (review)
Factorial definition:

5 factorial = 5! = 5 * 4 * 3 * 2 * 1 = 120
</pre>

<pre>

Sample Input: [3,6,1,3,2]

Sample Input: [3,6,1,3,2, 10]

Sample Input: 5 * 6
